MINATO NAMIKAZE

Minato Namikaze was the fourth hokage of konohagakure in the naruto verse.
He had the titles Yondaime which meant the fourth due to his being the fourth hokage and the
Yellow Flash of the Leaf. He is considered to be one of the fastest ninjas in the series. He
is the father of the main character of the show, Naruto Uzumaki.

Lifestory

Minato was considered to be a prodigy who completed the ninja academy in recoerd time beaten only by Itachi
Uchiha. He aspired to be hokage from a young age hoping to be admired by others due to being an orphan himself.
It was in the academy that he met Kushina Uzumaki (his future wife). He was specially trained by Jiraiya, one of
the legendary sannin due to Jiraiya recognizing his genius. As an adult, he was feared greatly in the third great
ninja war as an individual who showed no mercy and who should be avoided at all cost. His proficiency in killing
and achievements in the war caused him to be given the title of hokage(a chief of sorts). These abilities of his
were what made him feared;

  • Rasengan(a spiralling blue ball of chakra)
  • Toad sage mode
  • His unnatural speed
  • His large chakra pool
  • Teleportation
  • Summoning techniques
  • His tactical genius
  • Nine tails mode
